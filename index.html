Rules and Guidelines

Rules

  • Theme: Each artwork must depict one virtue: Courage (Lion), Focus (Cheetah), Patience (Leopard), Sharing (Giraffe), or Unity (Zebra).
  • Upload: Submit via Bazar to the Savanna Legacy Sprint collection (dd/mm/yyyy – dd/mm/yyyy).
  • Tags: Include Virtue, Title, Tools, and a short intent statement (50–100 words).
  • Standards: Content must be wholesome, respectful, and aligned with the virtues. Hateful or explicit content will be disqualified.
  • Mada: Kila kazi ya sanaa lazima ionyeshe fadhila moja: Ujasiri (Simba), Kuzingatia (Duma), Subira (Chui), Kushiriki (Twiga), au Umoja (Pundamilia).
  • Pakia: Wasilisha kupitia Bazar kwenye mkusanyiko wa Savanna Legacy Sprint (dd/mm/yyyy – dd/mm/yyyy).
  • Lebo: Jumuisha Fadhila, Jina, Zana, na taarifa fupi ya nia (maneno 50–100).
  • Viwango: Maudhui lazima yawe ya heshima, ya kuhamasisha, na yaendane na fadhila. Maudhui ya chuki au ya wazi yatakataliwa.

Guidelines

  • Originality: Only original works are accepted.
  • Timeline: Submissions open dd/mm/yyyy and close dd/mm/yyyy.
  • Permanence: All entries are stored on Arweave for lasting cultural impact.
  • Community: Share your process and support others with #SavannaLegacySprint.
  • Asili: Ni kazi za asili pekee ndizo zinakubaliwa.
  • Ratiba: Maingizo yanapokelewa kuanzia dd/mm/yyyy hadi dd/mm/yyyy.
  • Udumu: Maingizo yote yanahifadhiwa kwenye Arweave kwa athari za kudumu za kitamaduni.
  • Jamii: Shiriki mchakato wako na uunge mkono wengine kwa kutumia #SavannaLegacySprint.
